About Me

I am a music composer and sound designer from London, currently based in South Korea. I have an MSc in Sound Design and a BMus (Hons) in Music, from the University of Edinburgh. I previously studied on a music scholarship at Eton College and was a boyhood chorister at Westminster Abbey. I specialise in composing, recording, mixing and mastering soundtracks for all types of media, and enjoy writing with sensitivity with the hope of evoking and channeling complex human emotion.

I love working on new things! If you would like to enquire about music for a project, please contact me at andrewgalvinmusic@gmail.com.
